Terms of Service

These terms explain how AuraWebs handles project scope, approvals, payments, revisions, and ownership for client work.

Project Scope

Each engagement begins with an agreed scope, timeline, and deliverables. Requests outside that scope may require an updated timeline or quote.

Client Responsibilities

Clients are responsible for supplying accurate content, brand assets, approvals, and access credentials needed to complete the project.

Payments

Invoices are due according to the proposal or contract. Work may pause if payments become overdue.

Delivery and Ownership

Final deliverables are transferred after full payment unless a different arrangement is stated in writing. AuraWebs may showcase completed work in its portfolio unless confidentiality terms prevent it.

Support

Post-launch support, maintenance, and optimization are provided only when included in the agreement or purchased separately.
